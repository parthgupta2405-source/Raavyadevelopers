About Us

Built on a foundation of trust & craftsmanship

Founded in Boston in 1998, Cornerstone Group has grown from a regional contractor into one of New England's most respected construction and real estate development firms. Our integrated model means we bring architectural vision, construction expertise, and investment discipline under one roof — delivering better outcomes for clients, communities, and our built environment.

We believe the structures we build are a reflection of our values. Every project, regardless of scale, is approached with the same commitment to quality, transparency, and enduring impact.
